Empowering PCB's inspection through AI-powered image analysis

Who We Are

We develop AI-driven systems that analyze PCB images to detect faults like missing parts, wrong soldering, or broken traces.

Our Mission

To automate PCB inspection using deep learning, reducing manual effort while improving precision and reliability.

Our Vision

To make PCB defect detection fully automated through AI-powered image analysis, ensuring zero-defect production and smarter quality control.

Key Capabilities

AI-driven PCB defect detection that identifies faults and highlights them in PCB images.

What Circuit Sherlock Predicts

Mouse Bite

Detects jagged, punched-like patterns.

Spur

Predicts spiky/branch-like shorts.

Missing Hole

Spots missing or incomplete drilling.

Short

Finds unintended bridging paths.

Open Circuit

Detects interruptions and incomplete paths.

Spurious Copper

Finds random, unwanted copper patches.
